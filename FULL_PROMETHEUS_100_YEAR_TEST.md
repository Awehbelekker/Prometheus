## 🎯 Metrics Being Calculated

### Performance Metrics
1. **Total Return**: Overall return over 100 years
2. **CAGR**: Compound Annual Growth Rate
3. **Annualized Return**: Average return per year
4. **Final Value**: Capital after 100 years

### Trading Metrics
1. **Win Rate**: Percentage of winning trades
2. **Number of Trades**: Total trades executed
3. **Winning Trades**: Count of profitable trades
4. **Losing Trades**: Count of losing trades
5. **Average Profit**: Average profit per winning trade
6. **Average Loss**: Average loss per losing trade

### Risk Metrics
1. **Sharpe Ratio**: Risk-adjusted return
2. **Max Drawdown**: Maximum peak-to-trough decline
3. **Volatility**: Portfolio volatility

### System Metrics
1. **Average Decision Time**: Speed of decision-making
2. **Decisions Made**: Total decisions processed
3. **System Efficiency**: Overall performance
