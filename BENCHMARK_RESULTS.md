## Known Limitations and Mitigations

### 1. API Rate Limits

**Issue**: Free APIs have rate limits (Alpha Vantage: 5/minute, 500/day)  
**Impact**: Slower scanning during peak times  
**Mitigation**: 
- Caching of recent data
- Intelligent request batching
- Premium API upgrade option

### 2. Market Hours

**Issue**: Stock market data only available during trading hours  
**Impact**: Reduced opportunities outside 9:30 AM - 4:00 PM ET  
**Mitigation**:
- 24/7 crypto trading
- Pre-market and after-hours scanning
- International market coverage

### 3. Capital Requirements

**Issue**: Multi-strategy approach requires more capital  
**Impact**: Minimum ~$1000 recommended for effective diversification  
**Mitigation**:
- Adjustable strategy allocation
- Single-strategy mode for smaller accounts
- Paper trading for testing
