## 💰 **Trading Features**

### Basic Trading

#### Placing Orders
1. Navigate to the **Trading** section
2. Select your desired stock symbol
3. Choose order type:
   - **Market Order**: Execute immediately at current price
   - **Limit Order**: Execute at specific price or better
   - **Stop Order**: Execute when price reaches stop level
4. Enter quantity and price (if applicable)
5. Review and confirm your order

#### Order Types
- **Buy**: Purchase shares of a stock
- **Sell**: Sell shares you currently own
- **Short Sell**: Sell borrowed shares (Live Trading only)
- **Buy to Cover**: Close a short position

#### Portfolio Management
- **Positions**: View all current holdings
- **Performance**: Track gains/losses over time
- **Diversification**: Monitor portfolio allocation
- **Risk Metrics**: Assess portfolio risk levels
